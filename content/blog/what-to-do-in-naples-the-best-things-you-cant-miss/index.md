### ‚úà Murale Diego Armando Maradona

A true delight for football lovers ‚Äî the place isn‚Äôt very large, as it‚Äôs in an open space, but it‚Äôs well appreciated by both locals and tourists.

**The Diego Maradona Mural** is one of the most iconic street art pieces in Naples, located in the Spanish Quarter (Quartieri Spagnoli).
